Privacy Policy — Bounce Connect

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Overview

Bounce Connect is a local network tool that bridges your Android device and Mac. We do not collect any personal data.

What We Don't Do

Bounce Connect does not: collect or transmit data to external servers, track your location, share data with third parties, or use analytics or advertising SDKs.

How It Works

All data — notifications, messages, clipboard, calls, files — travels exclusively between your Android device and your paired Mac over your local Wi-Fi network. It never leaves your network and is end-to-end encrypted.

Data Storage

The app stores only a pairing token (to identify your Mac) and a temporary notification cache — both locally on your device. Nothing is stored on any remote server.

Children's Privacy

Bounce Connect is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
